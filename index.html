My Story

Welcome to my professional journey - an odyssey spanning 17+ years in the dynamic realm of Information Technology & expertise across multifaceted sectors, including Fintech, Credit Lending, Cards, Digital Payments, Government Advisory, and the Aviation Industry. Currently I have donned the hat of a Fintech Product Manager, innovating a world-class Card Issuing & Authorising solution. I take pride in my last stint as a Senior Advisor to the Government of Madhya Pradesh, contributing significantly to Digital Transformation, Investment Promotion, Branding, and Marketing Strategy Advisory here.

Educationally, I hold a Post Graduate Diploma in Business Management from Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS) and a Bachelor of Commerce & Computers from Barkatullah University. Beyond the boardroom, my love for music fuels my creative side, with proficiency in guitar and piano, and a penchant for crafting electronic melodies.

My commitment to continuous learning and personal development has been the compass guiding my professional growth. Leveraging years of domain experience, I now specialize in designing cutting-edge Global Fintech, Payments, Credit Lending and Card Issuing Solutions.

My Ham Radio call sign is VU2JDC (Old:VU3PXG) and handle is Zeda. I love this hobby as it embarks me on the thrilling journey of operating an Amateur Radio station. I am also a proud Member of The Amateur Radio Society of India (ARSI). This hobby serves as my electronic oasis, allowing me to quench my thirst for knowledge and indulge in captivating experiments. Eager to master the intricate art of Morse Code and delve into the fascinating realm of Digital Modes, I'm ready to amplify my skills and explore the boundless possibilities of amateur radio.

In essence, my journey is a testament to the fusion of strategic acumen, technological prowess, and a passion for creativity & innovation. As I continue to orchestrate solutions at the intersection of technology and finance, I remain committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the ever-evolving landscape of Information Technology!
